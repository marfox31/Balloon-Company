Health and Safety.


Tom does not really worry about the health and safety aspect of flying. His main theory is "What goes up, must come down." But there are some stipulatiions he adheres to.


  • There is no lost and found propery department.
  • No age restictions, but parents/gaurdians must take back their children who have flown.
  • Free Falling from the balloon is welcome, it saves on fuel of the return journey.
  • There is no set duration of a flight as Tom never knows when its time to give up.
  • BLT sandwiches are allowed but without the B.
  • Hail, Rain or Snow Tom will still go up.
  • Tom does not care about any kind of wind, including yours. So you can let rip all you like.
  • There are no toilet facilities you should go before you take off.
  • Do not try and feed the birds because Tom will throw stones at them. (Tipperary people are called 'Tipperary Stone Throwers')
  • All items such as hats, wigs and eyelashes should be secured with strong glue.
